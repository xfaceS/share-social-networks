This plugin is really useful for bloggers who want to share their content on social networks. The plugin allows you to share any post.

== Description ==

This plugin is a socials networks aggregator for WordPress.

This plugin has been developed for those who want to share their posts on social networks, but do not have the time or resources to do so. It is also for those who want to share their posts on all of the most popular social networks in one place.
Social networks are the most popular way to connect with people from all around the world.
The plugin will allow you to share your posts on different social networks without any additional work.
You will be able to share your blog posts and pages on social networks like Facebook, LinkedIn, Pinterest, VKontakte, Tumblr, Reddit or Twitter with just one click.

== Installation ==

1. Download Share Socials Networks.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Enjoy.
